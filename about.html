About Us

The Mahindra Thar is a compact, four-wheel drive, off-road two-door SUV manufactured by Indian automaker Mahindra and Mahindra Ltd. It was introduced in October 4, 2010 and replaced the Mahindra Major. It is the latest iteration in Mahindra's line of licensed CJ-3B Willys Jeep based off-roading oriented vehicles.

Mission

The mission of the Mahindra Thar is to offer a rugged, adventure-ready SUV that seamlessly blends off-road capability with modern comfort and technology. Designed for enthusiasts who seek freedom and thrill, the Thar is built to conquer challenging terrains while providing a refined driving experience on city roads. With its iconic design, powerful 4x4 performance, and advanced safety features, it continues Mahindra's legacy of durable and reliable SUVs. Whether for off-road expeditions or urban adventures, the Thar embodies the spirit of exploration, encouraging a lifestyle of adventure, resilience, and limitless possibilities.

Vision

The vision of the Mahindra Thar is to be the ultimate symbol of adventure, freedom, and off-road excellence while evolving with modern technology and sustainability. Mahindra envisions the Thar as a vehicle that inspires exploration, empowers enthusiasts to push boundaries, and remains a benchmark for rugged capability. With a commitment to innovation, safety, and environmental consciousness, Mahindra aims to make the Thar a global icon in the off-road and lifestyle SUV segment, continuously adapting to the needs of future generations while staying true to its legendary heritage.
